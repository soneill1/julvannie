ABOUT ME

Julvannie holds her Bachelor's degree in Psychology from Stockton University. She is a certified yoga teacher (YTT 200) in both Hatha and Vinyasa styles. She acquired her certification in Ballet Barre to supplement her yoga practice. Julvannie is also a Certified Massage Therapist for 10 years. She incorporates her background in Psychology, on how the brain functions, and in depth studies in anatomy from massage school to be an effective teacher. Julvannie is passionate about connecting the mind and body. She believes that taking care of our mental health is as important as taking care of our physical health, and following this principle is the hallmark of living our optimum life. While Julvannie holds certifications in other fields, she considers herself a yoga teacher more so because yoga can access this body/mind connection. It also encourages connection with the community, and a sense of inclusion. There's no strangers in Julvannie's book rather that we are all friends and family.
